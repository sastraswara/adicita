The Author¶

I am Sastraswara, the author of these essays. Thank you for coming. Probably you have read or skim some of the materials here. I hope that you find something for yourself.

If you find to give a commentary to what you read, feel free to write me an email. This can range from criticism, suggestions, ideas, et cetera. I will answer as soon as I am able.

This is one of many personal projects I am working on. Throughout my life, I realized that I have benefited from a lot of things. I see my works as a way for me to contribute back to society and to life. You are welcome to see my other works, starting from my front page.
